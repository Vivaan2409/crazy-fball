Features

Everything You Need

Professional-grade analysis tools, powered by state-of-the-art computer vision.

Real-Time Player Tracking

YOLO v8 detects and tracks every player on the pitch frame by frame, with persistent ID assignment across the entire video for accurate per-player statistics.

Auto Team Assignment

K-means clustering on jersey colors to automatically assign players to their respective teams.

Ball Possession

Track who controls the ball at every frame with precise proximity-based assignment.

Speed & Distance

Calculate real-world speed and distance covered using homography-based view transformation.

Camera Movement Compensation

Our optical-flow based estimator compensates for camera panning and zooming, ensuring accurate position tracking even with broadcast footage that constantly moves.
